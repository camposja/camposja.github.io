Father, Family man, Environmental & Running Enthusiast


Who am I?

Hello, my name is Jose. I am a father, brother, son & family man. I am passionate about the environment and I believe this comes from also loving outdoor activities. I am a running enthusiast who enjoys the exercise as much as the friends that I’ve made running. My son motivates me to improve my life and to set the best example possible.


I am a technology enthusiast who believes technology can be very exciting and useful. I believe in technology’s ability to improve people’s lives.


Background & Strengths

I am originally from Guatemala and now consider myself equally American and Guatemalan. Being originally from Guatemala I am a native Spanish speaker. I also speak little bits of Portuguese and Italian. I enjoy learning about languages and cultures because of the opportunities it gives you to make connections with people and its ability to help you grow as a person as well. Travelling, being a tourist and enjoying the scenery is not so bad either.


I earned a degree in Economics and worked for many years as a Financial Analyst in Banking. My previous career in banking has definitely given me my attention to clients needs as well the ability to work in teams. I am looking to find opportunities to marry my prior work experiences with my new skills as a Web Developer. Recently I completed a 12 week bootcamp style program in Backend Engineering at The Iron Yard in St. Petersburg, FL.


Goals

My main goal is to continue to grow as a Developer. I would like to work as a Fullstack Developer, Backend Engineer or another similar role which builds these skills. My main strengths and training are in Ruby and Ruby on Rails, JavaScript and related technologies. I am looking for an environment in which I can learn more about Software, Web Development and the tech business in general. I am open to opportunities that require my current skill set as a web developer and that might also take advantage of my skillset in Economics, Finance & Banking.
