About Me



Father, Family man, Guatemalan, American & Running Enthusiast


Who I am?

Hello, my name is Jose. I am a father, son, brother & family man. I am passionate about the environment and enjoy outdoor activities. I am a running enthusiast who enjoys the exercise as much as the social aspect of running. I believe that technology can be very exciting useful and I believe in it’s ability to improve people’s lives.


I enjoy myself on a challenging bike ride with my girlfriend as well on relaxing evenings at home. My son motivates me to improve my life and to set the best example possible.


Background & Strengths

I am originally from Guatemala and am proud of both my Guatemalan and American cultures. Being from Guatemala I am a native Spanish speaker. I also speak a little bit of Portuguese and Italian. I enjoy learning about languages and culture because of the opportunities it gives you for self grow. Travelling and being a tourist is not so bad either.


I earned a degree in Economics and worked for many years as a Banking Financial Analyst. I am looking to find opportunities to marry my prior work experiences with my skills as a developer.


Goals

My main goal is to continue to grow as a Developer. I would like to work as a full stack developer or back end engineer. My main strengths are in Ruby and Ruby on Rails. I want to be in an environment in which I can learn more about Software and Web Development and just the business in general. I would also be open to opportunities that require my current skill set as a web developer as well as my skillset in Economics, Finance & Banking. Eventually, I am very interested in learning other programming languages (maybe JavaScript, Angular JS, C# & .Net) and increasing my technological skills.
